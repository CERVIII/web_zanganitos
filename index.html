Generator Stamps

The Stamp Generator Project is an application developed in C that allows you to generate a customized number of images in PNG format. This application is based on the use of a folder containing several layers, which are randomly combined to create a single image file.

Zanganitos Collection

A 'Zanganito' is a round, animated character distinguished by its friendly personality and unique appearance. Their soft, rounded body, as well as their big eyes and friendly smile, make them endearing and irresistible. Each Zanganito has his own personality and distinctive traits that make them unique, but they all share an adventurous spirit and friendly attitude. These characters are ideal for collecting, trading and sharing with friends and family. If you're looking for an adventurous companion to make you smile and keep you company in your funniest moments, a Zanganito is definitely the perfect choice.
